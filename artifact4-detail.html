Key Learnings & Insights

Multiple Approaches Required

Real-world data challenges need complementary approaches rather than single solutions. Ensemble methods often outperform individual techniques.

Domain Knowledge Critical

Healthcare, automotive, and manufacturing each have unique constraints that inform solution selection and implementation strategies.

Missingness as Feature

Missing data patterns can be informative, especially in sensor systems where failure modes are predictable and meaningful.

Temporal + Spatial

Combining temporal consistency (Kalman) with spatial robustness (Vision Transformers) creates superior results for noisy vision systems.

Adaptive vs Uniform

ADASYN's adaptive approach better handles real-world manufacturing defects than uniform SMOTE oversampling methods.

Metrics Beyond Accuracy

For imbalanced data, F1-score, AUC-PR, and MCC provide better insights than accuracy alone for model evaluation.

Safety Considerations

Safety-critical systems require explainable methods alongside performance metrics for regulatory compliance and trust.

Production Trade-offs

Balance between accuracy, interpretability, and computational efficiency is crucial for successful deployment in production environments.
